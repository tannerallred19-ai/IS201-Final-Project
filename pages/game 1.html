Rules

Tron dies if he runs into his own trail. Bots also crash on their own trails unless shielded. Cutting riders off still scores kills and pushes the match into faster tiers every 2 eliminations.

Tron 5000 shifts the game toward pure trail warfare: denser pressure, dual spawn clocks, and shared power-ups.
